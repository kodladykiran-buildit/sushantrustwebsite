Who We Are

Rooted in Coastal Karnataka. Driven by Purpose.

Headquartered at Kodlady, Kundapur taluk (Udupi), with a focused commitment to Byndoor and Kundapur communities.

Sushan Trust is a family-driven philanthropic initiative dedicated to fostering education and innovation in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) and Environmental Studies. We believe education and environmental awareness are cornerstones of societal progress, and we exist to empower individuals and initiatives that carry these values forward.

  • Support academic excellence in core STEM and environmental disciplines.
  • Encourage innovation to address real-world environmental challenges.
  • Empower contributors and talented individuals through recognition and mentorship.
